## üöÄ How to Use

### Starting Your Practice

1. **Open the Application**
   - The app loads with a greeting message
   - Wait for questions to load (~1.5 seconds)

2. **Grant Microphone Permission**
   - Browser will ask for microphone access
   - Click "Allow" to enable recording

3. **Start First Question**
   - Click "Start Question 1" button
   - Recording begins automatically (text questions)
   - Or video plays first (media questions)

---

### During Practice

#### Text Questions

1. **Question appears** ‚Üí Recording starts automatically
2. **Speak your response** ‚Üí Audio levels show you're being recorded
3. **Click "Next Question"** ‚Üí Saves recording and loads next question
4. **Repeat** for all questions

#### Media Questions (YouTube Videos)

1. **Question appears with video**
2. **Click play button** on the video (‚ñ∂Ô∏è)
3. **Watch the video** ‚Üí Recording starts automatically when video ends
4. **Speak your response** ‚Üí Audio levels show you're being recorded
5. **Click "Next Question"** ‚Üí Saves recording and loads next question

---

### Navigation Controls

#### During Recording

**Pause Button:**
- Click to pause recording
- Click "Resume" to continue

**Next Question Button:**
- Saves current recording
- Loads next question immediately
- Shows "Loading..." during transition (~0.4 seconds)

---

### After Completing All Questions

1. **Review Section Appears**
   - See all your recordings
   - Play back each response
   - Read transcripts (if supported)

2. **Download Options**
   - Download individual recordings
   - Download merged audio file (all questions)

3. **Retry Option**
   - Click "Retry" to start over
   - Previous recordings are erased

## üêõ Troubleshooting

### Recording Not Starting

**Check:**
1. Microphone permission granted?
2. Microphone connected and working?
3. Browser supports audio recording?

**Solution:**
- Refresh page
- Grant microphone permission
- Try different browser

---

### Video Not Playing

**Check:**
1. Internet connection stable?
2. YouTube accessible?
3. Video URL valid?

**Solution:**
- Refresh page
- Check internet connection
- Try different video

---

### Transcript Not Showing

**This is normal if:**
- Using Firefox, Safari, or older browser
- Speech recognition not supported

**Solution:**
- Use Chrome or Edge for transcripts
- Audio still records normally

---

### Button Not Responding

**Check:**
1. Is button disabled (grayed out)?
2. Is transition in progress?

**Solution:**
- Wait for transition to complete (~0.4 seconds)
- Don't click rapidly
- Refresh if stuck

## üí° Tips for Best Experience

### Before Starting

1. **Test Microphone**
   - Check audio levels when recording starts
   - Adjust microphone position if needed

2. **Quiet Environment**
   - Find quiet space
   - Minimize background noise
   - Close windows/doors

3. **Good Internet**
   - Stable connection for video questions
   - Not required for text questions

---

### During Practice

1. **Speak Clearly**
   - Normal speaking volume
   - Not too close to microphone
   - Avoid shouting or whispering

2. **Watch Audio Levels**
   - Green bar shows you're being heard
   - Adjust volume if too low/high

3. **Use Pause if Needed**
   - Pause to think
   - Resume when ready
   - No penalty for pausing

---

### After Practice

1. **Review Recordings**
   - Listen to your responses
   - Read transcripts
   - Identify areas to improve

2. **Download Important Ones**
   - Save recordings you want to keep
   - Download before closing tab
   - Recordings lost when tab closes

3. **Practice Again**
   - Click "Retry" to start over
   - Try different questions
   - Track your improvement
