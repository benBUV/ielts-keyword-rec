# IELTS Speaking Practice App - User Guide

## Getting Started

### First Time Setup
1. **Grant Microphone Permission**: When you first use the app, your browser will ask for microphone access. Click "Allow" to enable recording.
2. **Check Browser Compatibility**: The app works best on Chrome, Edge, or Safari. Make sure you're using an up-to-date browser.

## How to Use the App

### Starting a Practice Session

1. **Loading Screen**
   - When you open the app, you'll see: "Hi, just a moment while I prepare your question(s)"
   - Wait a few seconds for the questions to load

2. **Ready Screen**
   - You'll see: "Ready? Click start to begin"
   - Click the "Start Question 1" button when you're ready

### During Practice

#### Preparation Time
- You'll have **5 seconds** to read the question
- A countdown timer shows the remaining preparation time
- Recording starts automatically after prep time ends

#### Recording Your Response
- **Audio Level Bar**: Shows when you're speaking (bars light up in orange)
- **Speaking Timer**: Displays how long you've been speaking
- **Target Duration**: Shows the expected speaking time for this question type

#### Question Types
- **Part 1**: Speak for about 20 seconds (short answers)
- **Part 2**: Speak for about 2 minutes (long monologue)
- **Part 3**: Speak for about 1 minute (discussion)

### Smart Features

#### Automatic Prompts
The app helps you stay on track:
- **5 seconds of silence**: "Still thinking? Practice expanding your ideas!"
- **10 seconds of silence**: "Still here? Let's move on" (recording stops)
- **No speech detected**: "No response detected" (shows Resume button)

### Navigation Controls

- **Stop Recording**: Manually stop your current response
- **Next Question**: Move to the next question (saves current recording)
- **Resume Recording**: Try again if no speech was detected

### Review Your Responses

After completing all questions, you'll see:
- **"Well done! Review your response(s) below"**

For each question, you can:
1. **Play/Pause**: Listen to your recording
2. **View Transcript**: Read what you said (if speech recognition is available)
3. **Download**: Save individual recordings

#### Download Options
- **Download Individual**: Click "Download" on any question to save that recording
- **Download All**: Click "Download All" at the top to get a merged audio file with all responses

### Starting Over

Click the **"Retry"** button to:
- Erase your previous attempt
- Return to the beginning
- Practice again with the same questions

## Tips for Best Results

### Audio Quality
- Use a quiet environment
- Speak clearly and at a normal volume
- Keep your microphone close but not too close
- Avoid background noise

### Practice Strategy
- Read questions carefully during prep time
- Speak naturally and continuously
- Don't worry about perfection - focus on fluency
- Use the full speaking time for each question

### Technical Tips
- **Microphone not working?** Check browser permissions in settings
- **No transcript?** Speech recognition may not be available in your browser (audio still works!)
- **Audio bars not moving?** Speak louder or check microphone settings
- **Recording stopped unexpectedly?** Check if you paused for too long

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**"Failed to start recording"**
- Check microphone permissions
- Make sure no other app is using the microphone
- Try refreshing the page

**"No response detected"**
- Speak louder or closer to the microphone
- Check if microphone is muted
- Click "Resume Recording" to try again

**Audio playback not working**
- Check your device volume
- Try using headphones
- Refresh the page and try again

**Transcript not showing**
- Speech recognition may not be supported in your browser
- Your audio recording still works fine!
- Try using Chrome for best transcript support

## Privacy & Data

- All recordings are stored **only in your browser**
- Nothing is uploaded to any server
- Recordings are deleted when you close the browser or click "Retry"
- Downloaded files are saved to your device only

## Keyboard Shortcuts

## Support

If you encounter any issues:
1. Try refreshing the page
2. Check your browser is up to date
3. Verify microphone permissions
4. Try a different browser (Chrome recommended)
5. Contact your instructor or IT support

## Best Practices for IELTS Preparation

### Part 1 (20 seconds)
- Give direct, clear answers
- Add one or two details
- Don't over-explain

### Part 2 (2 minutes)
- Cover all bullet points
- Speak continuously
- Use the full 2 minutes
- Organize your thoughts during prep time

### Part 3 (1 minute)
- Give extended answers
- Provide examples
- Show critical thinking
- Connect ideas logically
